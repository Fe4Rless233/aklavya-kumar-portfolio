Dedication to Service

A deep-seated commitment to community service is a cornerstone of my identity. I believe in giving back, actively contributing to the well-being of others, and leveraging my skills to support vital local initiatives.

Central Pennsylvania Food Bank

A Decade of Sustained Support

For over ten consecutive years, I have been passionately involved in fundraising for the Central Pennsylvania Food Bank. This vital organization works tirelessly to alleviate hunger, providing food to more than 253,000 people monthly. My long-term engagement has taught me the power of sustained effort and the significant impact dedicated individuals can make on pressing societal issues. It's deeply rewarding to contribute to strengthening our region's hunger relief infrastructure.

Local Robotics Coaching

Inspiring Future Innovators

For the past two years, I have dedicated my time to coaching robotics teams within my school district, working with enthusiastic middle and high school students. In this mentorship role, I guide aspiring engineers in robot design, construction, programming, and strategic problem-solving. It's incredibly fulfilling to witness their growth, foster their passion for STEM, and help cultivate the next generation of innovative thinkers and collaborative leaders.

Cultural Engagement

My cultural background is an integral part of who I am. I actively participate in sharing and celebrating my heritage, promoting diversity, and building strong community bonds through shared experiences.

Association of Bengalis in Harrisburg Area (ABHA)

Junior Patron & Cultural Ambassador

As a Junior Patron of ABHA, I play an active role in supporting vibrant annual cultural events that celebrate Bengali traditions, music, dance, and cuisine. These events also serve as important platforms for community outreach and fundraising, successfully raising over $300 each year for cultural programs and charitable initiatives. My involvement, including co-hosting, has been deeply enriching, allowing me to contribute to preserving our heritage while fostering a spirit of community and generosity.

Promoting diversity and fostering cross-cultural understanding through shared celebration.

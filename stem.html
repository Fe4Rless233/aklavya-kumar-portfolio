FTC Team 14464 BotBusters

My passion for STEM finds its most dynamic expression in my role with FTC Team 14464 "BotBusters." Since joining in our rookie year (2018), I've grown as a Lead Programmer, architecting solutions that drive our competitive edge.

Team Website Team Instagram

Lead Programmer & Innovator

My primary focus is on developing robust autonomous routines using Java and crafting versatile, strategic code for tele-operated segments. This involves intricate algorithm design, sensor integration (cameras, distance sensors, IMUs), and precise motor control to navigate complex game fields and execute tasks flawlessly under pressure.

Key Contributions:

  • Autonomous Systems Specialist: Designing and implementing complex algorithms for robot navigation and task completion.
  • Programming Strategy (Java): Collaborating on effective code for driver-controlled periods and endgame scenarios.
  • Mentorship: Guiding younger team members in programming concepts and robotics best practices.
  • Technical Portfolio & Presentation: Articulating our design process, innovations, and outreach efforts to judges.
